    • SELLEX, high quality & Scandinavian design approach

      SELLEX was founded in San Sebastián in 1977, and right from the beginning it has devoted all its efforts to the promotion of design furniture and equipment.

      A clear goal and dedication is to create “Scandinavian inspired” products, in collaboration with the most relevant Spanish designers.

      SELLEX continued and increased its work in this line during decades and SELLEX has acquired a significant experience in the contract market and equipment of public areas, such as Universities, Hospitals, Hotels, Airports, so on…, both in Spain and indeed abroad, and its high quality products are being distributed in more than 40 countries around the world.

      SELLEX has obtained a fair number of recent prizes and important awards to its work on favor of design.

      Link to Sellex website: www.sellex.es

  • WHAT WE DO: | WHAT WE OFFER?

    • Be objective and focus on the essentials

    • THIS IS US:

    • Image Collection was established in 2001 as a Nordic agency- and distribution company.

      Owner Thomas Larsen have years of knowledge as CEO from the contract furniture industry, since 1990.

      Image Collection sells via leading architects and distributors at the contract furniture market, whom decorate both public and private spaces in the Nordic countries.

      Image Collection’s brands are carefully selected after thoroughly research. Their service and visions must be of same understanding as Image Collection to operate in the Nordic markets. If they fail so do we.

      Since 2001 the road have been with retailshops, center showrooms at trademart to engaging large Copenhagen Furniture Festivals. 

      Today Image Collection are at Interior Post Cph with 12 other recognized brands and manufactores.

      Image Collection believes in a few keywords to succes for all:
       - easibility, painless co-operation and service.

      This is the only way our clients as resellers can obtain complete fulfillment in order to meet the present market demands from our common end-users.

      “We need to be with our clients and leads all the time. The loyalties we enjoy here are our keys to success. We know this and therefore we constantly try to adapt and apply to the on-going requests from our dedicated dealers, architects and interior designers”

       

      Thomas Larsen, Image Collection
